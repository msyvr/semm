Sign In With Ethereum - how it works:
  • Clicking the button triggers a request to MetaMask.
  • MetaMask checks the request and passes it to you.
  • You click to accept.
  • Your wallet credentials are passed to SIWE.
  • Your digital identity is verified* for login.
